Steak Recipe

Description

This is to get you started on the best steak

Ingredients

  • Olive Oil
  • Butter
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Steak
  • Garlic
  • Thyme

Steps

  1. First take out your steak 30 minutes before cooking and let it sit room temp
  2. Get the fan sizzling hot with little bit of oil
  3. Sear and cook both sides for 4 minutes each, seasoning both sides
  4. Add the butter and thyme and baste the steak
  5. Let it rest and serve
